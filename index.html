Seamless Video Publishing for Creators

FunUpdates is a local synchronization engine that helps creators auto-distribute their raw video updates directly from Telegram source environments to their authorized TikTok channel feed instantly.

See How It Works

Product Core Features

⚡ Automatic Syncing

Polls your private Telegram group, downloads media content securely, and schedules publishing immediately without manual intervention.

📁 Filename Cleaning

Intelligently filters out system names and raw metadata formats from captions to keep your public descriptions polished and professional.

📦 Spread Upload Groups

Combines adjacent updates and multi-part clips into unified batch pipelines, preserving resources and keeping your feed organized.

Three Steps to Automate

01

Link Source Channel

Configure the app config with your Telegram Bot Token to automatically monitor video additions.

02

Authorize TikTok

Securely link your TikTok account using standard OAuth login protocols. Tokens are stored locally only.

03

Run Engine

Start the background distribution script. Your updates are now continuously synced to your TikTok profile.
